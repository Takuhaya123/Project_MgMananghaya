Why did you choose to make this the theme of your site?

As a kid, I've always immersed myself in automobiles, and I always found them truly complex and beautiful. During the quarantine, F1 was something I discovered that aligned with such interests, so I watched and learned about it. My knowledge was minimal, being a fan for only 3 seasons, my knowledge is lackluster compared to someone who has been watching for longer. I decided that it would be a good website topic to expand my knowledge, and it is also something I find enjoyment in.
